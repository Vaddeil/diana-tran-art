About Me

Hi! My name is Diana and I am a local artist in Calgary.
I recently found courage to create again after a 8-year hiatus,
realizing that art makes me happy and will always be a part of who I am.
My current medium is resin.
I love making ocean-inspired pieces by combining multiple layers of resin with pigments and other mixed media.
As you can see, resin is very versatile and is not just limited to a canvas.
Here are some of my work so far!
